Tornadoes Visualization

This is a visualization of tornado data between 1950-2021 in US.

Tornadoes are one of nature's most powerful and destructive meteorological phenomena.
Due to their destructive potential, tornadoes are closely monitored and studied by meteorologists
The following visualisation showcase the data from Kaggle detailing the location, date, length and damage etc.
As shown in the visualisatin, tornadoes mostly occur in the east coast and especially central of United States.
Tornado alley is the central region of Uniterdd States receives high frequency of tornado.
The main reason is because moist Gulf air meets cold, dry Canadian air, it creats ideal conditions for severe weather
